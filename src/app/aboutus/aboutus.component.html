About Rosewood

Established in 2020 Rosewood is a distinctive collection of highly individual restaurant inspired by the culture, history and geography of each locale. Rosewood is an American Grill that offers a comprehensive menu of American fare with an Indian touch, featuring our exclusive Tex-Mex spread.We outdoor seating areas and 1 elegant indoor seating area with a live kitchen access for you to see us work our magic. Rosewood is your home for great times in downtown Pune.
